Life is Movement
based in London

Photographer & Videographer

Hello there! I am a photographer and videographer who has a passion for exploring and capturing unique moments. My love for photography began with street photography, which has become my main hobby.

Street photography is a great way to capture the essence of life in a city or town. It allows you to capture candid and authentic moments that you may not have been able to see otherwise. The beauty of street photography is that you can capture the hustle and bustle of everyday life, from the people to the buildings, without any preconceived notions. It is a form of art that is both challenging and rewarding, and it always keeps me on my toes.
‍
Apart from being a photographer and videographer, I am also a full-time web developer. I enjoy the challenge of coding and designing websites that are both functional and visually appealing. Combining my skills in photography and web development, I have been able to create unique and creative online experiences for my clients.

Overall, I am always looking for new ways to capture and showcase the world around me, whether it be through my lens or through my code.

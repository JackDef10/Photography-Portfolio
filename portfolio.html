About

I work as a web developer during the day, and in my free time I explore places that I've never been to with my camera. Ever since I started taking pictures, I've been looking for ways to expand my new found skill into something creative. Street photography aims to capture everyday life in public places, particularly in urban landscapes. Usually it's a form of candid photography, when the person isn't aware they're being photographed, which creates more realistic and powerful images.
